Whom is this training program meant for?

This training program is meant for experienced testers with limited tech and coding background. You will find this course useful if you:

  • feel your technical skills are getting outdated
  • are looking to gain practical experience with Python, Selenium, API testing, DevOps basics
  • have little scope to learn new technology at your current job
  • prefer training that has minimal amount of theory
  • like learning by doing practical challenges
  • are looking for a job change

We consider you an experienced tester if you:

  • have lead the testing effort on your team
  • managed and co-ordinated the testing of big releases
  • mentored junior QA
  • have at least 6 years of experience as a QA
